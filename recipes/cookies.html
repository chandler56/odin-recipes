Chocolate Chip Cookies

A classic chocolate chip cookie recipe with browned butter

Ingredients:

  • 2 sticks of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of sweet cream
  • 3/4 cup of dark brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 cold eggs
  • 3 teaspoons of vanila extract
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • a tiny bit of cinnamon
  • even less nutmeg (like a very very small amount)
  • 2 teaspoons of diamond salt
  • chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Brown the butter only until it becomes fragrant. Add the sweet cream and mix periodically until cooled and creamy
  2. Add the sugar. Mix well until whipped.
  3. Add the eggs. Mix in one at a time and fully incorporate
  4. Add vanilla. Make sure everything is mixed well and whipped together.
  5. In a seperate bowl, mix together all of the dry ingredients.
  6. Add in the chocolate. Its a good idea to crush/chop it a little to add chocolate swirls
  7. Prepare baking sheet with parchment paper. Scoop out the cookie dough in 1 tablespoon-1/4 cup balls. chill for at least 12 hours.
  8. Bake at 350 for about 16 minutes. Let cool on sheet for 5 minutes.
