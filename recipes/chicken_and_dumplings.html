Chicken and Dumplings

A classic southern specialty

Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 diced carrots
  • 2 diced celery stalks (with leaves)
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 1/4 cup of all purpose flour
  • 4 cups of low sodium chicken broth (see recipe)
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 2 cups of cooked shredded chicken
  • extra salt and pepper (to taste)

Instructions:

  1. saute the carrots, celery, and onions in 2 tablespoons of butter in a large pot over medium high heat until softened a bit and beginning brown (about 3 minutes).
  2. add 1/4 cup of flour, stir to coat.
  3. add the chicken broth and bring to boil.
  4. turn heart down to simmer and add 1/2 cup of milk and the chicken.
  5. meanwhile, to make the dumplings, mix the remaining 1 cup of flour, 1 teaspoon of salt, 1 teaspoon of baking soda, and italian seasoning. stir in 2 tablespoons of butter, melted, until mixture is crumbly looking. add in 1/2 cup of milk and stir until JUST combined. DO NOT over mix.
  6. add spoonsfuls of the dumpling mixture to the simmering pot, and cover for 15 minutes.
  7. salt and pepper to taste.
